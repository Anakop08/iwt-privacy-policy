# Privacy Policy for Interval Workout Timer

**Last updated:** January 12, 2026

## Introduction

Interval Workout Timer ("IWT", "we", "us", or "our") operates the Interval Workout Timer mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data We DO NOT Collect

Interval Workout Timer is designed with your privacy in mind. **We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.** Specifically:

- **No user accounts** - The app does not require registration or login
- **No personal data** - We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any identifying information
- **No location tracking** - We do not access or track your device location
- **No analytics** - We do not use analytics services that track your behavior
- **No advertising** - We do not show ads or use advertising networks
- **No third-party services** - We do not integrate any third-party tracking or data collection services

### Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

- **Workout preferences** - Your customized interval durations, cycle counts, and sound/vibration settings
- **App settings** - Your display preferences and notification settings
- **Usage statistics** - Local counters for app launches and completed workouts (used only for showing optional rating prompts)

**Important:** All data is stored exclusively on your device using Android's local storage (SharedPreferences and DataStore). This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app
- Cannot be synced or backed up to cloud services

## Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions to function properly:

### Required Permissions

- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS** (Android 13+)
  - Purpose: Display workout progress notifications
  - Used for: Showing timer updates and workout controls on your lock screen
  - Data collected: None

- **FOREGROUND_SERVICE** and **FOREGROUND_SERVICE_HEALTH**
  - Purpose: Keep the workout timer running when the app is in the background
  - Used for: Ensuring accurate timing when you minimize the app or lock your screen
  - Data collected: None

- **WAKE_LOCK**
  - Purpose: Keep screen on during workout (if you enable this option)
  - Used for: Preventing screen from turning off when "Keep Screen On" is enabled
  - Data collected: None

- **VIBRATE**
  - Purpose: Provide haptic feedback for interval changes
  - Used for: Vibrating at the start and middle of each interval
  - Data collected: None

All permissions are used solely for core app functionality. No data is collected, transmitted, or stored as a result of these permissions.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no personal data that could be compromised. The local preferences stored on your device are protected by Android's standard app sandbox security model.
